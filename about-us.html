Who We Are?

Based in Trabzon, B-MAK Engineering & Architecture provides professional solutions in heating, cooling, insulation, and mechanical installation. We blend our expertise in mechanical engineering with a modern architectural perspective to add value to your projects.

As an authorized dealer of industry-leading brands such as Baymak, Austrotherm, and Marshall, we deliver engineering services that combine efficiency, durability, and aesthetics.
